Welcome to the Open Council Data Platform

How it works

  1. Councils: publish data to your favourite open data portal, following the Open Council Data standards.
  2. We aggregate the datasets here by topic: garbage collection zones, dog walking zones, public toilets, and so on.
  3. Developers: you can build apps that use this data directly. When a new council publishes data in the right format, your app automatically works for that council.
